Luminaire

Designing a zero-fixing lamp

Look below

Background

Xicato delivered a brief which was half-open and half-closed. They wanted either a standalone lamp or a wall lamp. I chose to develop a desk lamp with a twist.

I know a few people who struggle to adjust the position of a lamp because they have arthritis or poor motor abilities in their hands and fingers. This means that grabbing anything small becomes challenging for them. If I could produce a lamp that had reduced components and was easy to assembly then this would truly benefit the day-to-day of these people, especially if they use a lamp for their office/desk. Moreover, a lamp like this would be a pleasure to use amongst all abilities – Solving a problem for one solves a problem for all.

My brief was to develop a lamp that had zero-fixings, minimal components making it easy to assembly, and adjustable. This would help to tackle the issues faced by those who have poor dexterity in their hands and fingers.

Sustainable

This product is made up of 4 components. The Light Unit, Stem, and Base (Left & Right) have been designed to minimise waste material in the manufacturing stage and importantly the materials are all sustainably sourced. For example, the wood comes from FSC Forest Stewardship Council certified distributors and the metal heatsink is recycled aluminium – Recycled aluminium required 95% less energy to process than virgin material.

Simplistic

A flat-packed design making it more efficient and cheaper to ship more units in the same container compared to competitors. Furthermore, the assembly on the users' end is as simple as Lego without being able to put a piece in the wrong place like a jigsaw.

Next

Stylish

The product can be produced from different types of wood or even other materials. Because of the minimalist nature of the product, there was not any room to route the cable internally. Though attempts were made in the prototyping stages I deemed the best approach to make the cable a feature, to give it a bit of colour and make it customisable so the user can have a product which reflects them.
