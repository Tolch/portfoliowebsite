Background

Avery WePrint is a massively growing subsidiary of Avery and has proven itself with now over a million orders. The success is down to the modernisation and optimisation of their online presence.

However, to get to this stage required analytical and critical review and a revamp of WePrint’s website. This included: updating and managing their file system and breadcrumb, replacing old poorly-Photoshopped images with fresh and aesthetic images displaying their products, improving SEOSearch Engine Optimisation, cleaning-up code, and so much more.

Whilst working for Avery, myself and 2 other talented individuals overcame all sorts of problems. I developed as a web designer and developer and moreso as a project manager, focusing on WePrint’s UXUser Experience and UIUser Interface elements along with SEO. I provided needed assistance in regards to coding and ensuring that all website functions were performing successfully.

Coverpage Ideation

During my time at Avery WePrint, I also took on the responsibility of our social media account. My first call of action was to come up with a grid-based theme that we can use. I also wanted to get out customers on our social platform because they provide us with great content for relatively little effort. This method of approaching our customers for use of their images/designs proved to be so popular that this formed the foundation of what the Avery WePrint UK Instagram is today. I managed to, within 6 months of taking this project on, get our following from 100 followers to over 2,000 people.
