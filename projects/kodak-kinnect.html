Kodak Kinnect

Military home communication

Background

Mother-child separation has detrimental impacts on both mother and child wellbeing and development. Typical deployment periods in the Armed Forces are 6 – 9 months, this limits the time that a mother can spend with her child. Children that do not have a mother in close proximity show increase activity in the amygdala which is connected to fear and stress. Mothers can be forced away from their new-borns in as little as 6 ½ months after birth.

This immediate separation completely ruins any chance of mother-child attachment which takes 3-years of quality time together to develop. Without a mother present in their infant and child years, children can grow up to develop attachment issues with family members, friends, and romantic interests as well as less likely to grow up to become happy, independent and resilient adults.

The project aim was to create a contextual product capable of providing revolutionary technology to help provide families with a better means of communicating with loved ones. The Kinnect is designed to help maintain parent-child relationships by providing a parent-presence in the home.
Disclaimer - This is a visionary product where the technology is currently in its infancy.

What it does

Currently no product is suited for the unique challenge’s military families face. Trying to maintain their connection over vast distances with limited talk time. Kodak Kinnect has been developed to seamlessly integrate into the home. Using existing military communication methods as a guideline, the Kodak Kinnect can transform any audio or message-based communication system into a 3D projection of that individual talking and interacting with you.

Next

Kinnect's Features

The Kodak Kinnect packs 4 volumetric projectors which produce the main image with 2 filler projectors to fill in rest of the projection. Packing an 8K camera, the drone can automatically send high-quality key moments to the away parent along with a transcript of the conversation.

Next

Bladeless Technology

Do you hear that? No? Well that’s the innovation of bladeless drones. The Kodak Kinnect navigates air around its body forcing it downwards which produces thrust with no noise.

Next

3D Projection

The Drone’s 4 main and 2 filler volumetric projectors can produce a full 360° projection. This creates depth to the projection which increases realism. The projection can be produced at 20-meters from the drone without getting distorted or encountering scaling issues.

Next

Intelligent Prediction

The Drone can predict what the output should be before the user has said a phrase making the conversation more natural and fluid for those viewing the projection.

Next

Smart Messaging

Intelligent predictions determine what should be outputted. The Drone analyses incoming messages and then acts them out as if the individual was really talking to you.
